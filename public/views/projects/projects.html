My projects

Games

Video games are the most complex form of art. They incorporate most of other art forms in one way or another. However the most important and unique ingredient - is interaction. And it is the hardest part. I love making games because of their tight relationship with the biggest and the most complex game of all times - life.

2d

What is a painting? In essence - it is a representation of ideas that can be expressed only through visual cues. It is something you have to master in order to share your ideas through art. And remember - your pencil is your weapon - so you should learn how to hold and use one. Sometimes, good concept art is all you need.

3d

In most cases you will need 3d skills to produce some decent game assets, a breathtaking trailer or that clever logo of yours. But there are so many different possibilities and workflows! There are industry standarts like Maya and Zbrush, and also lots of new kids on the block, like Blender and Quixel. And you have to know all of it!
